Frequently Asked Questions

Is delhi.rent free to use?

Yes, completely free. No signup, no login, no fees. We believe access to rental data should be open.

How is my privacy protected?

Your exact location is rounded to ~111m (3 decimal places) before sharing. Your contact info is only shown to people who click "I'm Interested" — and then only your email is shared, never your phone.

Who can see my listing?

Everyone. Your listing is anonymous and public. Only you can edit or delete it via your browser's device ID.

How do I edit or delete my listing?

Click on your own pin (shown with a special marker) and use the Edit or Delete buttons at the bottom of the popup.

Can I list a flat I'm looking for?

Yes — use "Looking for flat" mode. You can share your budget, BHK preference, gender preference, and lifestyle notes.

What do the pin colours mean?

Green = gated society rental, Orange = open/independent rental, Blue = seeker (looking for flat), Purple = flatmate needed.

What is the Green Cover overlay?

Sentinel-2 satellite imagery showing vegetation density. Bright green = dense trees/farms, useful for spotting quiet, green neighbourhoods.

How accurate are the metro lines?

Metro lines are approximate for display purposes. Zoom in to see actual station markers. Always verify with official DMRC maps.

How does "Watch This Area" work?

Enter your email and we'll notify you when a new listing appears within 1km of the pinned location for the duration you choose.

What happens if a listing is fake or spam?

Community members can report listings. Listings with 3+ reports are automatically hidden. We review reports regularly.

What's the difference between a broker and direct landlord listing?

"Direct" means the owner posted it themselves. "Broker" means a broker or agent posted it. Both are valid — just helps you know who to contact.

Can I use delhi.rent on mobile?

Yes, it works on any browser. For the best mobile experience, add it to your home screen — it behaves like a native app.

How do I share my listing?

Click on your pin, then "Share" — you can send a WhatsApp message or copy the link.

Is my data sold or used for ads?

No. delhi.rent is a community tool. We don't sell data, run ads, or use your information for anything except the service itself.

How can I contribute data?

Drop a pin on the map! Every listing helps the community. Share what you know — rents, neighbourhood quality, anything relevant.
