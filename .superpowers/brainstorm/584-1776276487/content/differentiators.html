What makes your NCR version distinct?

Multi-select — pick everything you want to include. These are Delhi NCR-specific features bengaluru.rent doesn't have.

🌫

AQI Overlay

Show live Air Quality Index by area on the map. Delhi's AQI is a real deciding factor for renters — no other rent map has this. Free API available (WAQI).

🏠

Builder Floor tag

"Builder Floor" is a massive housing category in NCR (South Delhi, Gurgaon, Noida) that doesn't exist in Bangalore. Add it as a property type alongside Gated Society / Apartment.

🤝

Direct Landlord vs Broker tag

Let pinners mark whether the flat is available directly from the owner or via broker. Massive pain point in NCR — renters specifically hunt for no-broker deals.

💧

Water Supply tag

Municipal 24/7, tanker-dependent, or borewell? A real differentiator in NCR areas like Gurgaon where water is a known problem. Community-reported.

🚗

Commute hubs filter

Filter pins by commute time to Cyber City, Connaught Place, Noida Sector 62, or any pinned office hub. Bangalore's metro filter but smarter — distance to work, not just a station.

📍

Sector-aware area labels

NCR runs on sectors (Sector 29 Gurgaon, Sector 62 Noida). Area stats and filters should speak sector language, not just locality names like Bangalore does.

⚡

Power cut frequency tag

Let renters report how often power cuts happen. Highly variable across NCR areas — a real quality-of-life signal that no listing site shows.

🛣

Expressway overlay

Show NH-48, Dwarka Expressway, Yamuna Expressway on the map. Major commute arteries that determine property value in NCR — like metro but for cars.

💡 Top picks for max differentiation: AQI overlay + Builder Floor tag + Broker tag + Water supply — these alone make it feel like a product built specifically for NCR renters.
